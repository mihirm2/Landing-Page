Personal Projects

Beyond research, I've channeled my curiosity and creativity into developing unique personal projects:

FootPredictor

Combining my love for football and statistical analysis, I created FootPredictor, a model that predicts the outcome of football matches using Poisson distribution. This tool not only forecasts game results but also provides odds for various outcomes, making it a handy companion for football enthusiasts.

Terminator.GPT

Inspired by the advancements in artificial intelligence, I developed Terminator.GPT, an AI chatbot that leverages the vast corpus of internet text to provide intelligent responses. This project, still in its final stages, aims to create a versatile and powerful communication tool that can become a leading AI resource.
